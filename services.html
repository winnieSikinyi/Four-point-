1. General contacting (project manager)

With our team of talented young professionals, we offer reassurance of running your project
efficiently, on budget, and on schedule. We understand that construction process can be a
complex undertaking and as such, we blend our human resource capacity with a construction
management system to inspire confidence, efficiency, and quality. Clients, consultants,
subcontractors and project managers can instantly share valuable information on the system to
ensure construction projects progress are are tracked using the integrated CMS technology.

Renovations and Additions

At Four point ventures we undertake all kinds of renovations and additions of all sizes, from
adding or redefining your living spaces, (Homes & Offices) to give them an audacious elegant

undertaking, but we are here to help and offer assurance of your project benefiting from
technical, hands-on experience, and expertise and expertise right from inception to completion. We will
execute your ideas to your satisfaction and offer suggestions where needed.

in detail, discuss project costs, working and walking with you from inception to completion.
